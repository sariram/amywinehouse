Amy Winehouse Best Live Compilation

Frank

Frank is the debut studio album by English singer and songwriter Amy Winehouse.

It was released on 20 October 2003 by Island Records. Production for the album took place during 2002 to 2003 and was handled by Winehouse, Salaam Remi, Commissioner Gordon, Jimmy Hogarth and Matt Rowe.
